EmeraldFox Fitness – Frequently Asked Questions

What is EmeraldFox?
EmeraldFox Fitness is a workout and client management app designed to help trainers organize workouts and track progress.

Do my clients need the app?
No. EmeraldFox is designed for trainers to manage their client workouts.

Where is my data stored?
Your workout and client data may be stored locally on your device and securely synced to cloud storage.

Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can remove clients, workouts, and other data directly in the app.
You can also request a full delete or account delete at any time by contacting EmeraldFox.

Is my data shared with other companies?
No. EmeraldFox Fitness does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Does EmeraldFox Fitness give medical advice?
No. EmeraldFox Fitness is only a workout tracking tool and does not provide medical or health advice.
