Privacy Policy FoxFit

FoxFit respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the FoxFit mobile application.

Information We Collect

FoxFit may collect the following information:

• Account information such as your email address and name 
• Client names you create within the app
• Workout data you enter (exercises, sets, notes, etc.)
• Basic technical information such as device type and app usage

FoxFit does not collect payment information and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

• Provide and maintain the FoxFit application
• Store and sync workout and client data
• Store your email and name for your user profile
• Improve app functionality and performance
• Provide support if issues occur

Your data is used only for operating the app. It is not shared with or sold to anyone

Data Storage

Your data may be stored:

• Locally on your device
• On secure servers used for syncing data

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Data Sharing

FoxFit does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.
Data may only be shared when required by law or necessary to operate the service.

Your Control Over Data

You may:

• Edit or delete your data inside the app
• Request a delete of your account at any time

Deleting the app may remove locally stored data.

To request data deletion, see contact

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app means you accept any updates.
